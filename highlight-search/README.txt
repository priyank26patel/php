Features:
---------
a) It highlights the searched words or text, instead of reading the full webpage.

b) Highlight matched words script is case insensitive.

c) Responsive and easy to place anywhere in your webpage.

d) Displays the matched words in yellow color.

e) Help the users to scan the webpage by highlighting keywords they are looking for.
